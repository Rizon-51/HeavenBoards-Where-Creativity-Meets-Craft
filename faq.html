Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

1. What types of boards do we sell?

We sell premium boards for interior design, furniture making, table tops, wall panels, cabinetry, and more.

2. Can I customize the size or finish of a board?

Yes, custom sizing and finishes are available on request for bulk orders.

3. How do I place an order?

Browse products, add to cart, and checkout after login or registration.

4. What payment methods do we accept?

Credit/debit cards, mobile banking (bkash, Nagad, Rocket), digital wallets, and bank transfers.

5. Do you ship nationwide or internationally?

We currently ship across Bangladesh. For international orders, contact support.

6. How long does delivery take?

3-7 business days depending on your location.

7. Can I track my order?

Yes, tracking information is sent via email/SMS after shipping.

8. What is our return and refund policy?

Return unused products within 7 days. Refunds are processed within 5-7 business days.

9. Is my personal information secure?

Yes, your data is encrypted and stored securely.
